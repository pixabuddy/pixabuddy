Who is Pixabuddy?

My name is Abhishek Saha, professionally known as Pixabuddy. I am a Graphic, UI/UX, Web Designer, and also a Front-End Developer based in Kolkata, India. The design has always been my passion and hobby since my early childhood. I have the curiosity to know-how designing and programming work together.
